The Worker should mark the work as "Finished" first. This acts as a formal delivery notice, signaling to the client that the task is done and they are ready for payment/review.
The Client then provides the final "Confirmation." By marking it as completed on their end, they verify the quality and release the feedback loop.
Why? This dual-check system prevents disputes. The worker proves they delivered, and the client confirms they are satisfied before the transaction is finalized.
